    Business Closure Registration

    1. Business Closure Notice:

    Important Notice:

    Before completing your business closure registration, we would like to remind you that this decision may affect your current transactions and contracts with customers, partners, and employees. Ensure that all financial obligations, contracts, and unfinished services are settled before proceeding with this step.

    Please note that after the closure, your services and products will no longer be available, and all financial transactions or contracts may be impacted.

    2. Customer Rights Notice:

    Customer Notification:

    You will need to clearly inform your customers about the discontinuation of services or products. This includes:

    • Refunding any unresolved payments.
    • Informing customers about the cessation of services/products.

    3. Post-Closure Support Notice:

    Support After Business Closure:

    After closing your business, you can still contact us at 19000617 to resolve any pending issues. We will assist you in completing legal procedures, handling contracts, and addressing any issues that arise during the transition or company shutdown process.
